When was Vodafone founded?

In 1982, Newbury, United Kingdom.
In 1998 Vodafone Egypt entered the Egyptian telecom market. Vodafone Egypt has grown over the years to become the leading mobile operator in Egypt, not only in revenue share but also to become the number 1 mobile operator in Egypt with the largest customer base. Vodafone proudly serves more than 36.3 million customers (December 2011) offering the most advanced technology for its customers, the best working environment for its 6,500 employees and the strongest corporate responsibility initiatives for the community.
Interesting piece of information: -
The name Vodafone comes from voice data fone (the latter a sensational spelling of "phone"), chosen by the company to "reflect the provision of voice and data services over mobile phones.

What programs and internships do Vodafone presents?

There is Vodafone’s Discover graduate program , which is a global accelerated program that enables talented fresh graduates to develop and grow within Vodafone and ultimately becoming Vodafone’s future leaders granting them challenging business exposure through; customized job rotation and continuous development.
Ana trainee is an internship program, which if you’re an undergrad student looking for a challenging and exciting experience, join Vodafone’s Internship Program and add some more excitement to your summer/winter.
You will get a unique opportunity to explore potential jobs and gain real hands-on experience with Egypt’s Top Employer. You can find yourself working across different business departments and receiving the right coaching and mentorship. Not to mention, you will also get front-row seats to experience the global Digital Telco business.
STEM program is one of the most powerful programs presented by Vodafone. This special program helps bright young girls to create, innovate, and learn about the fields of Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM). Vodafone Egypt is excited to offer this great opportunity for 90 girls (14 to 18 years old) throughout a period of 6 days, where the girls will learn how to code, create their own website and learn about the Tech industry.
